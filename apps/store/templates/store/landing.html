About Us


San Miguel's Exporta was established in 1995, we are ceramic professionals that have many warehouse locations located in Mexico. You order and we serve. We ensure to only bring the highest quality products made from the best materials and hand artists.

The rich Talavera pottery heritage was introduced to Mexico by Spanish guild artisans of the Colonial period. Commonly called "majolica" in Spain, Mexican Talavera draws its name from the 16th century Spanish pottery center, Talavera de la Reina.
